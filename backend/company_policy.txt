NEXUS DYNAMICS - EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK 2024

1. Remote Work Policy
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days a week. Mandatory in-office days are Tuesdays and Thursdays. If you need to work remotely for a full week, you must get written approval from your direct manager at least 14 days in advance.

2. Hardware Upgrades
Every employee is entitled to a hardware refresh every 3 years. The standard issue laptop is the Apple MacBook Pro M3. If your role requires heavy 3D rendering, you may request the M3 Max variant via the IT Portal.

3. Annual Bonus Structure
The end-of-year bonus is calculated based on company performance. For 2024, the multiplier is 1.15x. Bonuses are paid out on the last Friday of January. To be eligible, an employee must have been with the company for at least 6 months prior to the payout date.
